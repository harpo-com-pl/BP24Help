- New line: Dot 8 (Enter) or joystick action
- Backspace: Dots 7 (Backspace) or Space + dots 12 (Space + b)
- Previous line (paragraph): Space + dots 1 (Space + a) or joystick up
- Next line (paragraph): Space + dots 4, joystick down
- Previous word: Space + dots 2
- Next word: Space + dots 5
- Previous character: Space + dots 3 or joystick left
- Next character: Space + dots 6 or joystick right
- Beginning of note: Space + dots 1-2-3
- End of note: Space + dots 4-5-6
- Save without exiting: Space + dots 234 (Space + s)
- Save and exit: Space + dots 15 (Space + e)
- Exit without saving: Space + dots 14 (Space + c)
- Switch between computer/ literary Braille: Space + dots 1245 (Space + g)
